# Professional Networking Email Templates

## Cold Outreach Templates

### Template 1: Reaching Out to Someone You Admire

**Subject:** Quick question about your career journey

---

Hi [Name],

I hope this message finds you well. My name is [Your Name], and I'm currently [your role/situation]. I came across your [article/talk/profile] about [topic], and it really resonated with me.

I'm particularly interested in [specific aspect of their work], and I'd love to learn more about your journey to [their current position/achievement].

Would you have 15-20 minutes for a brief virtual coffee chat? I'd be grateful for any insights you could share.

Thank you for considering this request. I understand you're busy, so no worries if the timing doesn't work out.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[LinkedIn Profile]

- [Question 1]
- [Question 2]

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
[LinkedIn] | [Portfolio/Website if relevant]

## Pro Tips for Networking Emails

1. **Keep it short** - Respect their time; aim for under 150 words
2. **Be specific** - Reference something specific about them or their work
3. **Make it easy** - Suggest specific times or ask for a brief response
4. **Follow up once** - If no response after 1 week, send one gentle follow-up
5. **Add value** - Offer something in return when possible
6. **Proofread** - Typos and errors make a bad first impression
7. **Personalize** - Never send a generic mass email
